Privacy

Respect for people includes respect for their information.

This plain-language policy covers the static website, email forms, and future donor communication.

Information We Collect

If you contact the ministry, volunteer, or ask about giving, the site may collect the information you choose to send, such as your name, email address, interest area, and message.

How It Is Used

Information is used to respond to your inquiry, coordinate volunteer interest, discuss giving, and keep records needed for ministry accountability.

Sharing

Personal information should not be sold, rented, or shared for unrelated marketing. It may be shared with ministry team members or service providers only when needed to respond or operate the work.

Static Site Note

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. Temporary mail forms open the visitor's email app. A future form provider or donation processor may have its own privacy and security terms.
