## Identity

The archived `archeintl.org` site represented Arche' International, a Christian nonprofit/ministry founded in 2009 by Geoffrey and Kathy Hichborn and based in California, USA.

The organization was linked with Arche' Africa International, which the old site described as having headquarters in Fort Portal, Uganda.

## Original Positioning

The clearest original motto from the archived homepage was:

> Reaching Communities with the Essentials, and then with the Gospel.

The organization described itself as a nonprofit seeking quality-of-life improvements in communities around the world, beginning in Fort Portal, Uganda.

## Historical Focus Areas

- Clean drinkable water and restoring non-functioning wells.
- Training communities to maintain and repair wells.
- Micro-enterprise businesses for self-sustaining income.
- Medical, trauma, and birthing facility support.
- Training medical personnel and students through volunteer specialists.
- Support for vulnerable children, widows, orphans, and people with disabilities.
- HIV/AIDS counseling, shut-in visitation, tutoring, mentoring, and prayer.
- Gospel outreach, Bible distribution, and church/ministry partnerships.

## Places Mentioned

- Fort Portal, Uganda.
- Rwebisengo, western Uganda.
- Kampala, Uganda.
- Rwanda, Kenya, and Pakistan in later archived content.
