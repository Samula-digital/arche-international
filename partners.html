Partner Standard

What every partnership should clarify.

  • Who is responsible for decisions, funds, and reporting?
  • What project or community need is being served?
  • How are local leaders involved before, during, and after the work?
  • What photos, names, and stories can be shared ethically?
  • How will updates be sent to supporters?
