Who We Are

A nonprofit ministry shaped by partnership and practical care.

The archived story of Arche' International begins with people joining hands across borders so communities could receive essentials, encouragement, and Gospel-centered support.

Archive Story

Founded in 2009 with a helping-hand-up vision.

The IRS Business Master File lists Arche International under EIN 27-0796734, in care of Katherine Hichborn, at an Anaheim, California address. The archive describes the ministry's practical beginning with Geoffrey and Kathy Hichborn, local leaders, volunteers, medical workers, builders, and churches.

The work was never framed as one person carrying the whole mission. The archived language repeatedly pointed to shared gifts, shared accountability, and the belief that communities should be strengthened from within.

Partnership First
