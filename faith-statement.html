Faith Statement

Christian faith is the foundation of the work.

This is a concise draft based on archived material. Final language should be reviewed and approved by current ministry leadership before publication on an official domain.

Scripture

We believe the Bible is the inspired Word of God and the revealed will of God for faith, correction, and instruction.

God

We believe in the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit, eternally self-existent and working as one.

Jesus Christ

We believe redemption is found through Jesus Christ, the Son of God, and through His finished work.

Salvation

We believe salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ, not by human works.

The Holy Spirit

We believe believers should seek the empowering work of the Holy Spirit for Christian life and service.

The Church

We believe the Church is the Body of Christ, called to worship, witness, service, and the great commission.

Editorial Note

The archived site contained a longer doctrinal statement with several Scripture references. This rebuild uses a shorter version for clarity until current leadership confirms the exact wording to publish.
