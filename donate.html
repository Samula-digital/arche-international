Before Online Giving

Readiness checklist.

  • Create a hosted Stripe Payment Link, PayPal Donate link, Givebutter form, or Donorbox campaign.
  • Paste the live URL into `assets/js/payment-config.js`.
  • Confirm board approval for donation language and restricted fund handling.
  • Publish privacy, refund, and donor communication policies.
  • Verify the mailing address before listing checks or supply shipments.
