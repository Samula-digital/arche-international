FAQ

Clear answers before people give, volunteer, or partner.

These answers combine the archived site's recurring questions with practical launch guidance for the rebuilt ministry website.

Is Arche' International a nonprofit?

The IRS Business Master File lists ARCHE INTERNATIONAL with EIN 27-0796734. The transparency page summarizes the public record and the operational items still being configured.

Can I donate online right now?

Not yet. The site is ready for giving inquiries, but online donation processing should be enabled only after secure payment, receipt, privacy, and restricted-fund workflows are configured.

What makes the ministry different?

The archived site emphasized a helping hand up: practical support that strengthens local capacity instead of creating dependency. Programs should be planned with local leaders and reported transparently.

Where has Arche' International worked historically?

Archive records mention Fort Portal, Rwebisengo, Kampala, Uganda, and later connections to Kenya and Pakistan. Current active regions should be confirmed before fundraising for a specific project.

How can I volunteer?

Visit the volunteer page and send an interest note. Historical volunteer categories included medical, construction, administration, fundraising, printing, web, photography, and videography support.

Are archived contacts and addresses current?

The IRS filing address is published for transparency. Before sending checks, supplies, or sensitive documents, contact the ministry to confirm the best mailing or shipping instructions.
